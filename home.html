    About Us

    restaurant

    Freshly Cooked, Delivered in Real-Time

    Welcome to Taste by Shazia — a real-time home-based restaurant where every meal is made-to-order. As soon as you place your order, our kitchen starts preparing your dish using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations.

    Our system updates live order status, so you'll know exactly when your meal is being cooked, packed, and dispatched. No waiting in the dark—get real-time notifications via SMS or WhatsApp!

    Whether you're craving a quick chicken samosa or a full biryani feast, we cook it fresh the moment your order comes in. Our small-batch, just-in-time system ensures you never get reheated food.

    Meet The Chef

    Shazia is not just the heart of our kitchen—she’s also the brain behind our real-time operation. With over a decade of experience in authentic Pakistani cooking, she brings unmatched flavor to every meal.

    From preparing spice mixes to overseeing live orders, Shazia ensures every dish reflects homemade comfort, consistency, and a passion for food. She’s also quick to respond on WhatsApp and handles custom orders with care.
